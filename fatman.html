Fat Man (Fallout 3 Cocktail)

The Fat Man, mini-nuke. For when you just don’t have the time.

An explosive concoction of rum, whiskey, vanilla vodka and soda. This is one Fallout inspired cocktail that will blow you away!

Ingredients

  • ¾ shot Spiced Rum
  • ¼ shot Cinnamon Whiskey
  • 1 shot Vanilla Vodka
  • 6 shots Dr. Pepper

Steps

  1. Pour the Dr. Pepper in a highball glass and mix in the shot of vanilla vodka
  2. Pour the spiced rum and cinnamon whiskey into a shot glass
  3. Make sure bystanders are out of blast radius and deploy the fat man (er, drop the shot into the highball glass)
  4. Consume and make sure to find some Rad Away, quick!
